I recently started learning how to draw and I’ve been making some progress that I’m excited to share. I thought it would be helpful to create a space where I can track my progress and share any materials or other useful information that I come across along the way.

I think this could be a really valuable resource for anyone who’s interested in learning how to draw, whether you’re a beginner like me or someone who’s been doing it for years. I’m planning to post updates regularly, so feel free to check back in and see how things are going. And if you have any tips or recommendations for me, I’d love to hear them!

Thanks for taking the time to read this, and I hope you find the information I share to be helpful.
