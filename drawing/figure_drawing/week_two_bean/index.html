Week Two: Bean

Table of Contents

Join me as I unveil my artistic discoveries from the second week of Proko’s Figure Drawing Fundamentals course. In this post, I’ll share my progress and showcase how I learned to harness the power of the bean—a foundational element in capturing the human figure.

During this week, I delved into the fascinating concept of the “bean.” Acting as a simplified representation of the human form, it helped me capture gestures and movements with precision.

Take a glimpse at some of my finest drawings, where the bean played a pivotal role in capturing the dynamic beauty of the human figure.
