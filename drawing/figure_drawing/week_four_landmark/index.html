Week Four: Landmarks

2023-06-05 :: Maksym Postument :: 2 min read (301 words)
#Drawing  #Gesture  #Proko 

I can’t believe it’s already the end of week three, and now I’m eagerly stepping into the realm of week four! As I sat down to watch the theory video for this upcoming week, a sense of excitement mixed with a touch of trepidation washed over me. It became apparent that the challenges awaiting me in the days ahead are going to push my artistic skills to the limit.

One particular topic caught my attention—landmarks. These elusive elements of drawing have always perplexed me. How does one accurately depict and perceive them? It’s like entering uncharted territory, a realm where my artistic compass feels slightly askew. But I’m determined not to let that deter me!

In my quest for improvement, I’ve embarked on a journey with Proko, relying on his informative and illustrative example videos. They serve as my guiding light, illuminating the path towards understanding and mastering the art of drawing landmarks. By following along with Proko’s demonstrations, I hope to internalize the techniques and concepts, gradually building my confidence and honing my artistic intuition.

Sometimes, it takes a combination of theory and practical application to truly grasp a concept. Armed with newfound knowledge, I’m ready to immerse myself in the world of drawing, armed with pencils and sketchbooks as my trusted companions. With each stroke and line, I strive to unlock the secrets of landmarks and gain a deeper understanding of their significance in creating realistic and captivating artwork.

So, as I embark on this exciting and challenging week, I’m filled with a mix of determination, curiosity, and a hint of artistic vulnerability. But with Proko as my mentor and the joy of exploration fueling my creativity, I’m confident that I’ll make significant strides in my artistic journey. Here’s to pushing boundaries, overcoming obstacles, and discovering the artist within!
